  • ORIGIN OF OUR FABRICS

    We source all our deadstock fabrics in Europe — mainly Italy and France — choosing only recent, high-quality, fashion-forward textiles. By keeping sourcing local, we ensure beauty, relevance, and a lower ecological footprint.

  • CUT BY 3 METERS

    At Wasted Fabrics, all fabrics come in standard 3-meter cuts — the perfect length for most sewing projects. This helps us reduce shipping, keep prices fair, streamline our warehouse, and deliver the best fabric, fast.

  • WAREHOUSE

    Choosing fabrics is all about touching and feeling — that’s why you’re welcome to explore our warehouse in Drogenbos. Open Monday–Thursday (10–18h) and Friday (10–16h). Picking up an order? Discover hidden gems on our shelves! For groups or sewing classes, book an appointment online. We also host pop-up events in other cities — follow us on Instagram to stay updated.
