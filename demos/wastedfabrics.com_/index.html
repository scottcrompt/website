About

Curated deadstock, premium quality

We source designer overstock from European mills and brands and make it accessible to modern makers. Expect high standards, unique textures, and small-batch finds — perfect for projects that deserve character and story.

Designer-grade materials

From crisp poplins to fluid viscose, all fabrics are ex-designer deadstock with verified quality and hand feel.

Sustainable by design

Reduce waste and carbon by rescuing surplus textiles. Small runs mean less overproduction and more originality.

Standard 3‑meter cuts

Every piece arrives in a versatile 3 m cut — ideal for garments, draping, sampling, and student projects.

How it works: We source > Inspect & measure > Cut in 3 m lengths > You create.
