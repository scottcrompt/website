About Karst

Started in Sydney in 2016 with an original idea and genuine purpose, Karst set out to rethink paper by removing trees and water from the equation. The result is Stone Paper™ — a smoother, stronger, and more sustainable surface for ideas.

Stone Paper™ Innovation
  • Made from recycled stone (CaCO₃), not trees
  • Waterproof and tear-resistant
  • Ultra-smooth, low-friction writing
  • Brighter white without bleach or acids
